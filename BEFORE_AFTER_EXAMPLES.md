## ğŸ¯ The Pattern

### Every "After" Example Shares These Qualities:

1. **Opens naturally** - No "So, let's talk about..." or "Here's what's important..."

2. **Explains significance organically** - Built into the explanation, not called out with "this is crucial"

3. **Provides context** - Historical, technical, or business context woven in

4. **Quantifies when possible** - Specific numbers, percentages, timeframes

5. **Shows mechanism** - Explains HOW and WHY, not just WHAT

6. **Varies structure** - Each slide opens and flows differently

7. **Zero meta-instructions** - Never tells the speaker what to do

8. **Just explains** - Information speaks for itself
